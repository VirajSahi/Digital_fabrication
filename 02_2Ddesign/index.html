Modeling

I chose to model two objects: a key and a set of screwdriver bits. In retrospect, modeling the keys to my room wasn’t the best idea, since the key itself clearly says “PLEASE DO NOT REPLICATE.” (I really, really hope I don't get into trouble for this). Overall, it was a really fun process. The only challenge I faced was that my system lagged significantly during detailed rendering, which slowed me down a lot. Hence, I had to spend over 10 hours on just on these two models.
